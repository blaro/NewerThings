Nova

About Nova

Nova is a Marvel Comics superhero that has many iterations. (The one here is named Richard Rider). Nova got his power from an alien from the planet of Xandar. The alien, with his dying energy, empowered Richard Rider with the power of the Nova Corps, an intergalactic military/police force.
