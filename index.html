About Nova

Nova is a Marvel hero/vigilante that has many iterations. (The one here is named Richard Rider). Nova gets his power from an alien from the planet of Xandar, the alien empowers Richard, right before he (the alien) dies, with the power of the Nova Corps, an intergalactic military/police force.
